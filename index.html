About Me

From young age I was under the impression I was explicitly passionate about chess. Once introduced to programming, I realized what I had falsely identified as a passion for chess, was a passion for creative problem solving and applying solutions.

After realizing my passion, I knew it would be best realized in the world of software engineering. I have since attended App Academy, a highly selective software engineering program, where I dedicated over 100 hours a week honing my skills in full stack web development. Not only do I know how to build full stack applications now, but I have mastered the tools and techniques to learn new languages and stacks, so that I can adapt to any environment. I am seeking opportunities to put my skills as a problem solver and programmer to work.
